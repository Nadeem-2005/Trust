About Us

We at the VIT Student trust strive to make life easier for students at VIT Vellore. This is a trust which will be maintained by students, members 0f the student welfare club and the administration of VIT. Students donate Rs 10 a Month to the trust. The money collected will be used in covering medical expenses, be it a common cold or a fracture. As students will also be a part, it gives them the responsibility to be empathetic towards others and be aware of what our society needs.
